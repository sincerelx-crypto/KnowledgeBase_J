# Collegamenti Inglese ed Educazione Civica
**English — Modern Architecture**
- The hotel is rooted in the **Modern Movement**.
- Le Corbusier → **Villa Savoye** (Poissy, 1929-31), **Unité d'Habitation** (Marseille).
- **Five Points of a New Architecture:** pilotis, free plan, free façade, ribbon windows, roof garden.
- **International Style** → Mies van der Rohe, Walter Gropius (Bauhaus, Fagus Factory).
- Modernist city planning: concentric, sector, multiple nuclei, axial models.

**Educazione Civica**
- Sustainable urban vs rural lifestyles.
- Workplace safety on construction sites (sicurezza in cantiere).
